💡 Pro Tip

Duplicate readings on the same date & time are automatically skipped so your records stay clean. You can safely re-import without worrying about duplicates.

Auto Status Detection

Each reading is automatically classified as Low (<70), Normal (70–140), or High (>140) mg/dL — matching your MyDigiDia records.

Only CSV Format Works

Make sure you select CSV (not PDF or Excel) when exporting from mySugr. PDF exports cannot be parsed by this tool.

Your Data is Private

Your uploaded file is processed instantly and never stored on our server. Only the parsed glucose readings are saved to your account.
