What MyDigiDia Record Offers

A complete diabetes management platform built for patients, caregivers, and healthcare providers.

Record Management

Log blood glucose readings digitally with timestamps, reading types (fasting, post-meal, bedtime), and personal notes for complete medical documentation.

Health Analytics

Visualize glucose trends with interactive charts. Track HbA1c estimates, time-in-range percentages, and daily averages to understand your health patterns over time.

Secure Authentication

Hashed password storage, OTP-based password recovery, and session management ensure only you can access your sensitive medical information.

Real-Time Monitoring

Add glucose readings instantly and receive color-coded feedback — Low, Normal, High — so you always know your current status at a glance.

mySugr Import

Seamlessly import your complete glucose history from mySugr or any CSV-based diabetes tracker using our intelligent column-mapping import wizard.

User-Friendly Interface

A clean, responsive design works beautifully on all devices — desktop, tablet, and mobile — making diabetes management easy for patients of all ages.

Record Management

Complete Control Over Your Glucose Data

MyDigiDia's Records module is the heart of your diabetes management. Every reading you log is instantly categorized, timestamped, and stored securely for your lifetime health history.

  • Log multiple readings per day with reading type classification
  • Color-coded status: Low (below 70), Normal (70–140), High (above 140)
  • Add personal notes like meals, exercise, or symptoms
  • Full edit and delete capabilities for every record
  • Bulk import from mySugr CSV in seconds
Go to Records
Analytics

Understand Your Health Patterns

The Reports module transforms your raw glucose data into clear, actionable insights — helping you and your doctor make better decisions about your diabetes management.

  • Interactive glucose trend charts with date range filtering
  • HbA1c estimate calculated from your 90-day average
  • Time-in-range analysis: Low, Normal, and High percentages
  • Morning, afternoon, evening, and bedtime reading breakdowns
  • Exportable PDF reports to share with your healthcare provider
